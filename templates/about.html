About Us

Everything we do is about delivering

AI LEAD is a company that provides expertise in design, solutions and services related to ICT Services, Smart City Solutions, Energy Solutions, empowered by Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. We cater to the high end market including telecom operators, private companies, Utility Companies and Operators, Public Authorities and Healthcare Institutions.

We specialize but not limited in the areas of Emerging & Disruptive Technologies (Smart City & IoT Enablement & Monetization), Big Data Analytics & Complex Event Processing, Virtualized Wi-Fi Orchestration Platform, NFV Service Assurance Platform (NFVSAP), Services Monetization (Real-time Charging & Revenue Management), Contextual Campaign Management, Policy & Charging Rules Function, Operations Support & Customer Care, Services Enablement & Delivery

Our Story

AI LEAD was founded with a passion for innovation and a vision to transform the technological landscape. We recognized the growing need for intelligent solutions that could address the complex challenges facing modern cities, businesses, and individuals.

From our early days focusing on traditional electrical infrastructure, we evolved to embrace emerging technologies like artificial intelligence, machine learning, and IoT integration. This evolution reflects our commitment to staying at the forefront of technological advancement.

Today, we stand as a comprehensive technology partner, delivering everything from smart city infrastructure to cutting-edge holographic displays. Our journey continues as we explore new frontiers in technology, always with our clients' success as our primary objective.
